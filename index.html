Welcome to the world of knitting

Whether you're a first timer looking to start or a veteran of the craft, this site has all of the resources you need to get going.
New to the game? Start at the Tutorials page.
Looking for inspiration? Then the Patterns page is for you!

Relaxing


Knitting has been proven to promote relaxation and mindfulness amongst people of all ages.
It can help to relieve stress and anxiety and contribute to a calmer mindset.

More jumpers!


Who doesn't love a comfy knitted jumper in the frigid winter months?
Knit a jumper that suits your style and in the colours you want.

Helps with focus and concentration.


You'll be surprised how much time flies by when you're focused on the knit as it weaves along.

Keeps fingers flexible and pliable


Just to slow down the impending arthritis and keep your coordination in tip top shape for years to come!
The rhythmic movements without much strain on the joints helps keep your fingers in motion.

Still Interested? Good!

Here's just a taster of things that you can learn to make with the power of knitting and a little patience.

Sweaters

Who doesn't love a good cozy sweater in the frigid winter months? This, beyond socks, is probably the most common use for knitting out there.

Endless Socks!

So many socks. You can go from lace weight all the way to super chunky to your desired thickness.

Blankets

With so many options, including being able to just use up any and all scrap yarn from other projects, you'll find yourself never having to buy a blanket again.

Hats, Scarves and Mittens

Complete that cozy winter look with your own hats, scarves and mittens!

And this isn't even half of it. There is so much you can accomplish with the art of knitting, you'll be in love in no time flat.
